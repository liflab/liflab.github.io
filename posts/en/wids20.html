Three LIF students participate to Women in Data Science

2020/03/09 by Sylvain Hallé

Three doctoral students from LIF, Asma Razgallah, Rania Taleb and Massiva Roudjane, participated in the first edition of the Women in Data Science conference in Saguenay, held on March 2nd, 2020. The event coincides with the annual conference “Global Women in Data Science (WiDS)” which is held at Stanford University and in more than 150 regions of the world. The Women in Data Science conference aims to inform and inspire data scientists of all kinds around the world and to support women in the field.

Ms. Razgallah, Taleb and Roudjane each presented a poster summarizing the work they have done in their PhD, under the supervision of faculty from LIF. Congratulations to all three!
