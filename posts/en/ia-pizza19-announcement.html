Pizza Time with Industrielle Alliance

2019/01/29 by Sylvain Hallé

For its first public conference of the year, LIF welcomes the staff of Industrielle Alliance, who will come to Chicoutimi to talk about the various open interships for the Summer and Fall of 2019. This conference is open to everybody, but focuses in particular on students of the professional Masters’ program who will be looking for an internship at the end of their studies. *Pizza and beverages will be offered!**

  • When? Wednesday, Feburary 6th, 2019 at 11:45 AM
  • Where? Room P4-1000
  • Reserve your pizza by confirming your presence

Industrielle Alliance is an insurance company based in Québec City. For many years, it has offered students various internships in Computer Science, and has recently completed an R&D project with students and faculty from LIF, through funding from NSERC.

Bring your CV!
