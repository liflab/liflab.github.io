LIF becomes an Inria associate team

2020/02/03 by Sylvain Hallé

LIF researchers have obtained funding under the Inria Associate Team program in France. The Associate Team program aims to create and strengthen research collaborations between Inria components and international research teams.

First, Professors Sylvain Hallé and Raphaël Khoury received funding to start a sustained collaboration with Professor Yliès Falcone of Université Grenoble-Alpes.

Then, Professor Fabio Petrillo, also a member of LIF, participates in a second team project associated in collaboration with Nicolas Anquetil and Stéphane Ducasse from Inria Lille Nord Europe; Professor Yann-Gaël Guéhéneuc of Concordia University is the main sponsor of this project.

The projects are funded over a two-year period, and provide for numerous collaborative stays between the French and Quebec parties.
